Vocal for Local Campaign

Vocal for Local has become the latest trending slogan which has emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic.However, the idea behind this movement is not new.It finds its roots in the Swadeshi movement which was popularised in 1905 during the Indian independence struggle, developed and promoted by Mahatma Gandhi and the other great freedom fighters.

Agenda Behind The Campaign

This Campaign is a part of Atmanirbhar Bharat initiative started by the Prime Minister of India. Agenda of this campaign is to promote people to prefer buying Indian Goods over Foreign goods. This can include purchasing handmade products of indigenous artisans and craftsmen. Also, to improve the manufacturing sector of India, by starting to manufacture most of the foreign goods in the country itself and improving the supply chain of products starting from raw materials to supplying it to the citizens.

Why Should We Be Vocal For Local ?

  • As the Pandemic has disrupted the global supply chain, being Atmanirbhar gives us benefit of stopping ill effects of pandemic on the Indian Economy
  • It would create a conducive atmosphere that truly supports common people to implement their business ideas and establish their startup.
  • If local sales flourish, it can potentially replace the goods we buy from foreign-based companies that import their goods to India.
  • Supporting local businesses has not one but multiple benefits to both us as buyers, the sellers, and also the country as a whole.
  • Another key benefit is generating employment which is one of the biggest concerns country faces.
  • Last but not the least, it will make India self-reliant
