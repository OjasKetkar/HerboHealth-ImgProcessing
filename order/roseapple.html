Price: 65 Rs./- potted plant

Botanical Name : Syzygium jambos

INTERESTING FACT -

: Rose apple is highly rich in nutrients like protein, fiber, calcium, iron, vitamin A and vitamin C. Its bark contains an alkaloid compound Jambosine, which has therapeutic properties against diabetes. Organic compounds like betulinic acid and friedelolactone are also present in it.

The rose apple tree a shrub but is generally a tree reaching 25 or even 40 ft (7.5-12 m) in height, and has a dense crown of slender, wide-spreading branches, often the overall width exceeding the height.

How to grow: The seeds inside ripe fruit is used for planting. Dig holes that have one-foot length, width and depth each. Fill the holes with neem cake, bone manure and topsoil before planting. Saplings can be replanted after three months. Replant saplings in May and June. Proper sunlight will ensure better yield. Regular watering is required.
