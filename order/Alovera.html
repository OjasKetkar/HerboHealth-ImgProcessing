Botanical Name : Aloe barbadensis miller

INTERESTING FACT -

Aloevera Can Survive For Over 100 Years and it consists of 99% of water and 1% solid portions are known to have the most active nutrients

It belongs to Asphodelaceae (Liliaceae) family. It grows mainly in the dry regions of Africa, Asia, Europe and America. In India, it is found in Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra and Tamil Nadu.
Medicinal properties :
  1. Antioxidant and antibacterial properties
  2. Reduces dental plaque
  3. Accelerated wound healing, soothes burns
  4. Helps treat canker sores
  5. Reduces constipation
  6. Improves skin, prevents wrinkles
  7. Lowers blood sugar level
How to grow:
  1. Know when to transplant
  2. Give the plant adequate sunlight and warmth (8-10 hours of sunlight per day)
  3. Plant the aloe vera in well draining soil
  4. Cover the root ball when planting but do not let the leaves touch the soil
  5. Do not water for the first few days
